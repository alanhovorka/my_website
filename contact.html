My name's Alan Hovorka. I’m a senior journalistic data analysis and presentation major at Ball State University. I'm a reporter, coder and data journalist.

Previously, I worked at The Dubois County Herald as a HSPA Pulliam intern during the summer of 2016 and on news desk of the Evansville Courier & Press during the summer of 2015. Also, I've worked as the university administration reporter and social media editor for The Ball State Daily News.

My work focuses on government accountability, public records, public access to information and making data digestible. I love public records requests and am quite familiar with the various laws that govern public information across the country.
